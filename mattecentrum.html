Mattecentrum overhaul

Ongoing complete redesign of Mattecentrum's website, covering user experience, information architecture, content structure, and visual direction. The project addresses a wide range of audiences while deliberately prioritising students as the primary users.

Original landing page.

Initial research

Initial work focused on understanding Mattecentrum's mission, audiences, and existing content. We held stakeholder meetings, reviewed the current site, and analysed usage data to identify structural and communicative issues.

Audience complexity

Mattecentrum serves multiple audiences, including students, parents, teachers, volunteers, and other stakeholders. While all groups need to be supported, we chose to dedicate the first page entierly to the students.

Information architecture & content

The previous site required extensive clicking to understand the scope of available resources. A large part of the redesign focuses on restructuring content and information architecture to provide clearer overviews, fewer dead ends, and more predictable paths.

Ongoing work

The project is ongoing, with further iterations planned based on user testing and stakeholder feedback.
